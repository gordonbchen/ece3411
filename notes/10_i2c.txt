I2C (Inter Integrated Circuit) / TWI (Two Wire Serial Interface)
* 7 bit addr: 128 devices

data lines are high in idle (pullup)
* pull down is faster

BAUD = (F_CLK_PER / (2 * F_SCL)) - (5 + (F_CLK_PER * T_R / 2))

Read:
* transmit addr (WR)
* transmit command/register
* transmit addr (RD)
* receive data
* stop

Write:
* write addr
* transmit addr (WR)
* transmit command/register
* transmit data
* stop
